Tottenham Hotspur and the English Premier League

Tottenham Hotspur are a professional soccer team located in London, England and are a part of the English Premier League or "Prem". In this league there are some of the top teams throughout the entire world such as Manchester City, Liverpool, Chelsea, Arsenal, and of course Tottenham Hotspur as well. Amongst these top teams there are fifteen others which all together, complete the roster for the league. In many cases people exclude Tottenham from being categorized as an "elite" or "top" team in the world but being a Tottenham supporter myself I find this not to be the case. Although my club have not had the best luck in regards to winning any major titles or trophies they are still a prominent team in the league and deserve the respect of soccer fans around the world.
