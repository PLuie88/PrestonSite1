Arsenal

The direct and fiercest rivals in all of England and the Premier Leauge are none other than Tottenham Hotspur and Arsenal. This rivalry has dated back to over one hundred years ago when Arsenal moved their stadium into North London much to the distaste of Tottenham supporters throughout England as Arsenal moved in and built their stadium only a few miles apart from Tottenham encroaching on what many supporters believe as "Tottenham territory". Today, this rivalry has only become more and more bitter with the infamous Sol Campbell who switched sides from Tottenham to Arsenal in the worst way. Campbell whose contract was set to expire shortly, said that he would re-sign with Tottenham only to betray them and sign for their worst enemy Arsenal, winning many trophies with them instead. Although currently Arsenal have been more succsesful historically then Tottenham, it is about time for Tottenham to have some success of their own.
